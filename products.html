Tuition Master

Tuition Master is an online teaching platform for tuition classes and institutions to conduct online classes, manage payments and connect with students. System supports both Sinhala and English languages enabling students to easily navigate and connect with classes.

Tuition Master comes without any branding and a customised design for your requirements. Features of the system are Zoom Meetings, Attendance, Bulk SMS, Payment Gateways, Forum, Course Based Access and many more.
